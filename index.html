How to Play

  • You are dealt 9 question cards plus a Shield, Hint and Swap — 12 cards total
  • Click a card to select it, then press Play Card
  • Answer the question correctly to discard the card
  • Answer wrong and 2 new question cards are added to your hand
  • Use Action Cards for special powers
  • Empty your hand to win!
